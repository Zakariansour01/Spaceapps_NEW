Asteroid Fun Facts

Discover the mysteries of space rocks

01
The Asteroid Belt
Most asteroids orbit the Sun in a region between Mars and Jupiter called the asteroid belt. Despite popular belief, the belt is mostly empty space with asteroids millions of miles apart!
02
Dinosaur Extinction
A massive asteroid impact 66 million years ago caused the extinction of the dinosaurs. The asteroid was about 6 miles wide and created a crater over 110 miles in diameter!
03
Sizes Vary Greatly
Asteroids range from tiny pebbles to Ceres, the largest asteroid, which is about 590 miles across. That's roughly the size of Texas!
04
Valuable Resources
Some asteroids contain precious metals like gold, platinum, and rare earth elements. A single metallic asteroid could be worth trillions of dollars!
05
Near-Earth Objects
Scientists track over 30,000 near-Earth asteroids. NASA monitors these space rocks to protect Earth from potential impacts and studies them for future missions.
06
Incredible Speed
Asteroids can travel at speeds up to 75,000 mph! That's fast enough to circle the Earth in about 20 minutes. Their orbital velocities make them challenging to study up close.
